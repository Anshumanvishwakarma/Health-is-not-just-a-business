About the Owner
Committed to
Your Wellness

Anmol Agnihotri founded Vandana Medical with a vision to bring pharmaceutical-grade health products to the people of Lakhimpur Kheri and across India. With deep roots in the community and a passion for preventive healthcare, Anmol has built a trusted brand that thousands of families rely on for their wellness needs.

Under his leadership, Vandana Medical has grown from a local pharmacy to a full-fledged health platform with AI-powered guidance, premium product curation, and reliable delivery across India.
